Tacos

Description

Tacos are a staple of Mexican food culture. For centuries tacos have not only feed people in Mexico but people all around the world. A popular dish to make due to how easy and quick they are to make, it's no wonder why people all around the world enjoy them.

A taco consits of a meat of your choosing that is surronded by a corn or flour tortilla. A plethora of toppings can then be added. These include; Onions, cilantro, tomatos, salsa, and so many more! Honestly the choices of meats and toppings make these one of the most verstiales foods out there!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Meat of choice
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Salsa
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Warm toritlla on stove
  2. Place the warmed tortilla on a plate
  3. Place meat of choice on tortilla
  4. Gently fold tortilla over meat
  5. Add toppings of choice
  6. Enjoy!
