Lazy Mans Enchiladas

Description

is a Mexican dish consisting of a corn tortilla rolled around a filling and covered with a savory sauce. Enchiladas can be filled with various ingredients, including meats, cheese, beans, potatoes, vegetables, or combinations of all of these

A lazy mans Enchilada is a taquito that is drenched in Enchilada sauce and thrown in the oven for a few minutes. This makes a quick and delicous meal for you and your friends to enjoy

Ingredients

  • Box of taquitos
  • Green or red Enchilada sauce
  • Queso Fresco

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Remove Taquitos from the box and place in an oven safe container
  3. Evenly spread enchilada sauce on top of taquitos
  4. Place dish in oven for 15-20 minutes
  5. Remove dish from oven
  6. Add queso fresco on top of the enchilads and let them cool for 5-10 minutes
  7. enjoy!
