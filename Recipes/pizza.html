Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • A ton of cheese
  • Olives
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees
  2. While over is preheating roll out your pizza dough and evenly spread it out to make a 12" circle that is not too thick or thin
  3. Evenly spread out your pizza sauce on the dough
  4. Add your cheese on top of the sauce. Add as much as your cheese loving hear desires
  5. Evenly distribute your Pepperoni,Olives,and Mushrooms
  6. Place your pizze in the oven.
  7. Once edges are golden Brown and cheese is melted take it out of the oven
  8. Cooldown for 10-15 minutes
  9. Cut into slices and enjoy!
